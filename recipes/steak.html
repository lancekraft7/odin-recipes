Pan seared steak

The pan seared steak thatll surely take your breath away

Ingredients

  • slab o' beef
  • 1 cup o' butter unsalted
  • 6 whole peppercorns

Steps

  1. slap that meat on the pan on the grill
  2. put butter and pepper on steak while cooking on medium heat
  3. Flip twice
  4. enjoy
