Spaghetti

Spaghetti a la familia

Here we have a spaghetti recipe from a spaghetti chef

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles- 1 bag
  • tomato sauce- 1 can
  • ground beef- 1 lb
  • salt- 1 oz
  • pepper ground- 2 oz

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. Insert noodles
  3. stir constantly for 7 mins
  4. strain water from noodles
  5. stir into sauce and enjoy
